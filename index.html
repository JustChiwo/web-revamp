About Us

Founded in 2017, Bennett Tea was created with a mission to make premium, 100% organic teas accessible to tea lovers while promoting healthier lifestyles and sustainable living. We carefully source and blend high-quality loose-leaf teas from trusted growers around the world to ensure exceptional flavour, freshness, and quality in every cup.

Guided by a passion for wellness, sustainability, and outstanding customer service, we strive to create memorable tea experiences while building lasting relationships with our community and caring for the environment.
